Who We Are

We are a group of girls ranging from ages 16-17 who are dedicated to creating an open environment where everybody feels accepted. We were brought together by our participation in Girls Who Code, where we worked together to build this website.

(From left to right, Taseer, Dissy, Snigdha, Princess)

Become a Part of Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is a nonprofit organization dedicated to closing the gender gap in the technology industry. It was founded in 2012 by Reshma Saujani. We encourage all girls to find a Girls Who Code club near them and become a part of the sisterhood! Check your school, local library, or create one yourself!

Join the movement: https://girlswhocode.com
