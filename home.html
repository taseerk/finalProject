Our Message

This website was meant to educate people about different cultures, ethnicities, and religions. It's purpose is to fight ignorance and not to spread hate in the world as there is already too much of it. We want everyone to be an advocate for the education of misunderstood or misrepresented cultures, and not to spread hate. None of these articles were written to be offensive, but rather meant to be informative. We do not condone any sort of violence and will not stand for anyone using our website to spread hateful messages.

Equality & Pride

This website was created to fight ignorance through educating people about different cultures around the world. We want everyone to feel accepted and welcome in our community and to have pride in who they are and where they came from. We believe that everyone deserves to have a seat at the table and be able to express themselves freely.
